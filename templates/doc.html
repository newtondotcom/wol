Documentation on how to add an addons

Your content might be double-checked by our moderation team, plus we have your email address and your discord

If your file's host is not in the list, your add-on will be deleted.

Name: The name of the add-on (max 100 characters).

Image: URL to the image representing the add-on (max 300 characters), must be a landscape photo.

Description: A detailed description of the add-on.

Changelog (optional): A brief summary of changes made to the add-on.

Download Link: URL to download the add-on (max 300 characters).

Download Host: The hosting platform for the primary download.

Download Link 2 (optional): URL to an alternative download source (max 300 characters).

Download Host 2 : The hosting platform for the download 2(if provided).

Game: The associated game for the add-on.

Category: The category under which the add-on falls.

Note (optional): Any additional notes or comments about the add-on (max 1000 characters).

Date: The date when the add-on is submitted (auto-generated).

Link 1 and Link 2 must be different.

For MSFS and FSX, link 1 stands for part 1 and link 2 stands for part 2. For P3D, link 1 stands for P3D V4 and link 2 stands for P3D V5. For XPlane, link 1 stands for XP11 and link 2 stands for XP12.

You can take example on the existing add-ons of the site.

If you wish to delete an add-on, please open a Discord Ticket on our server.
